Why we?

Upon launch within the first two weeks, Wolverinu has accomplished being listed on CoinMarketCap, CoinGecko, Shibaswap, Fegex. Wolverinu reached 21,000,000 Market Cap within 9 days, acquired 4500 holders, hosted 5 AMA’s, and held various contests. It is safe to say that Wolverinu has plenty of potential and room to grow in the near future.

Our Vision

Launched on October 23rd 2021, Wolverinu’s mission is to bring order to the Ethereum blockchain and prove what a powerful community can achieve when united. Thanks to the continuous and thorough communication within the community, our holders’ questions are always answered thanks to the developers, admins and moderators of Wolverinu.
